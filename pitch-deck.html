EmpathAI

Practice Intelligence for Therapy
  • Turning patient check-ins into clinical insight

EmpathAI is the intelligence layer for therapy group practices. We turn a 30-second weekly patient check-in into structured session prep, practice health scores, and early-warning signals — so practice owners can actually see whether their practice is healthy before a therapist burns out or a patient drops off.
The Problem

Therapists Are Drowning

93% of behavioral health professionals report burnout.
  • One-third of therapist time goes to admin, not patients
  • 40% are considering leaving the field entirely
  • Practice managers have zero visibility into clinical health
The mental health workforce is short 31,000 practitioners and attrition now outpaces new entrants. A third of every therapist's week is paperwork. Practice owners can tell you how many appointments happened — they cannot tell you whether patients are progressing, whether caseloads are balanced, or which therapist is about to quit. Existing tools handle scheduling and billing. Nothing measures whether therapy is actually working.
The Solution

Practice Intelligence, Not Paperwork

One check-in. Two intelligence layers. Zero patient data exposed.
  • Patients check in weekly — 30 seconds, no app required
  • AI turns check-ins into structured session prep for therapists
  • Practice Health Score gives managers a single number to track
Patients enter a join code, rate how they're doing 1 to 10, optionally leave a note, and they're done. That feeds two things: first, an AI-generated session prep brief the therapist reviews before the next session — themes, trends, suggested focus. Second, a composite Practice Health Score that blends wellbeing, engagement, outcomes, and caseload balance into one number the practice manager watches weekly. Everything is privacy-first by design — no names, no diagnoses, no identifiable data ever reach the AI layer.
How It Works

Three Roles, One Workflow

Patient
30-second check-in via join code. Crisis detection built in. No app, no account.
Therapist
AI session prep with review gate. One-tap session ratings. No chart-diving.
Practice Manager
Practice Health Score. At-risk alerts. Caseload analytics. One dashboard.
Patient check-in  →  AI session prep  →  Practice Health Score
The patient experience is deliberately minimal — join code entry, a rating, an optional note. Crisis language is detected in real-time and surfaces a national helpline. On the therapist side, check-in data flows into a structured session prep card — rating trends, behavioral themes, a suggested focus area. But the AI content stays blurred until the therapist marks it reviewed. We never let AI output bypass clinical judgment. The therapist also submits a 30-second session rating after each session. All of this rolls up to the practice manager as a Practice Health Score — a weighted composite of patient wellbeing, session engagement, outcome progress, and therapeutic alliance.
